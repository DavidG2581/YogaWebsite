Explore Our Yoga Styles

Hatha Yoga

A gentle, slower-paced practice focusing on basic postures and breathing techniques. Perfect for beginners and those seeking relaxation.

Vinyasa Flow

Yin Yoga

Deep, passive stretches held for extended periods to target connective tissues and promote flexibility.

Restorative Yoga

Ultra-relaxing practice using props to support the body in restful poses for deep healing and stress relief.
